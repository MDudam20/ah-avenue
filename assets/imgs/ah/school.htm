Ryan Global School, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai About Us

Ryan Global School is a modern, technologically-enhanced, co-educational day school providing an international curriculum. Our school enables all individuals to realise their academic and personal potential in a dynamic and challenging learning environment which values enquiry, perseverance and reflection. We are a community which acts with integrity and treats one another with respect, learning together as responsible global citizens. Students thrive in a learning environment that provides support, challenge and encourages success. The characteristic �buzz� of excitement about learning, noted by many observers, is contagious. The students and teachers enjoy coming to school; they have positive attitudes to learning and consistently achieve more that they first thought possible! The school has been built to an exceptionally high standard, providing superb facilities appropriate for quality teaching and learning. Each classroom is fully air-conditioned and resourced to a very high level, including interactive whiteboards and WiFi connectivity. Specialist teaching areas for Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Music, Art, ICT and Media are available. As part of our sporting programme, the students have regular access to the swimming pool and the extensive outdoor sporting facilities. The highlight of any parental tour seeking admission for their child is the impressive and well-resourced Learning Resource Centre. Visitors to the school regularly comment on the friendly, family atmosphere which is achieved through recognising the importance of developing a meaningful partnership between the school, parents and students.
